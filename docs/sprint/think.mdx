The Think phase is about questioning assumptions before committing to a direction. Most projects fail not because of bad code, but because they build the wrong thing.

## Skills

| Skill | Role | What it does |
|-------|------|--------------|
| `/office-hours` | YC Office Hours | Six forcing questions that reframe your product. Design doc feeds into every downstream skill. |
| `/design-consultation` | Design Partner | Build a complete design system from scratch. Researches the landscape, proposes creative risks. |

## The reframe

`/office-hours` doesn't take your feature request at face value. It listens to your pain and extracts what you're actually building.

**Example:** "I want a daily briefing app" becomes "You're building a personal chief of staff AI" — because the agent listened to the pain (stale calendar data, bad prep, wrong locations) instead of the feature request.

## Design at the heart

`/design-consultation` creates the visual identity that flows through the entire system. It writes `DESIGN.md` — and then `/design-review`, `/plan-eng-review`, and every frontend agent respects it.
